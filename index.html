Why Clean Water Matters

Clean water changes everything. It improves health, education, and opportunity for families and communities.

  • 1 in 10 people lack access to clean water
  • Women and children spend 200 million hours daily collecting water
  • Clean water reduces disease and saves lives

The Water Crisis

785 million people lack access to clean water. This global crisis impacts health, education, and economies.

Health

Diseases from dirty water kill more people every year than all forms of violence, including war.

Education

Children miss school because they spend hours collecting water.

Women & Girls

Women and girls carry the burden of water collection.
